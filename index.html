Who We Are

Madurai's
Premier
Fabricator

M/s. A.S.R. Body Works is a Madurai-based heavy vehicle body fabrication company operating from Plot No. 17, 18, 19, Udhayam Nagar Main Road, Kovilpakudi – Madurai 625 018.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, we design, manufacture and supply purpose-built vehicle bodies to municipal corporations, government contractors, and private fleets across Tamil Nadu and South India.

We are a Government of India-recognised OEM under the NSIC Single Point Registration Scheme — certified to supply 21 product lines directly to Central and State Government agencies without earnest money deposit.
